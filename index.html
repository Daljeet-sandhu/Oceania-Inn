About Us

Oceania Inn offers delicious dining and takeout to Rochester Hills, MI.

Oceania Inn is a cornerstone in the Rochester Hills community and has been recognized for its outstanding Chinese cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff.

Our Chinese restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.
